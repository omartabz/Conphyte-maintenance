Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the Conphyte Maintenance Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) page! We understand that maintaining your property can sometimes raise questions, and we're here to provide clear and helpful answers. This page is designed to address common inquiries about our services, processes, and policies. Whether you're a new client or have been with us for years, we hope this resource will offer valuable insights and quick solutions.

What services do you offer?

  • We provide a range of maintenance services, including plumbing, electrical, HVAC, landscaping, and general repairs. We cater to both residential and commercial clients.

    • How do I request a service?

    • How do i pay for the services?

  • The total service fee is charged to your residential fee.

    • Can I hire my own maintenance outside of the buildings crew?
